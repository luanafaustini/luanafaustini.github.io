About Me
Thanks for the pic, Helen ♥

I'm a multidisciplinary senior product designer with a deep passion for cybersecurity, collaboration, and human-centric design.

My work consists in creating products that not only look great but also feel intuitive, secure, and meaningful to the people using them. For me, design is all about solving real problems—balancing usability, security, and sustainable growth in a way that benefits both users and businesses.

You can also find me hiking, biking and taking pictures, collaborating with , getting excited with new leaves of my plants, cooking and snugging my cat.

I’m always excited to work and collaborate with teams that share a vision for building thoughtful, ethical, and impactful products.
