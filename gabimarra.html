Client: Gabi Marra

Services: Visual Identity

Year: 2018

Gabi Marra is a beautician from Rio de Janeiro with a big responsability of taking care of all those gorgeous people. The concept was created using the Narcissus flower that carries the meaning of beauty and honesty in different cultures of the world. Gabi Marra is always improving herself professionaly with state-of-the-art techniques, so this fun-loving and meaningful visual identity was created to keep up with her good work.
