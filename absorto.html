Client: Igor Absorto - absorto.dev

Services: Visual Identity & Website Development

Year: 2020

Igor Absorto is a freelancer web developer. As part of his personality and as his job requires, he is always eager to learn more and keep growing knowledge day after day. So that was the main idea for this creation: resilience. The helix is present in a lot of elements in our day life symbolising resilience and strength. By replicating the helix several times it was possible to create this robust geometrical pattern full of depth. His website was all coded by me from scratch using only HTML & CSS.
