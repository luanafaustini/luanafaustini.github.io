Client: MAR Arquitetura

Services: Visual Identity

Year: 2019

Mar Arquitetura is a Porto Alegre-based office with a Bahian heart, specialized in interior design. Applied across a variety of projects, the logo had to be flexible: it is the intersection of the smootheness of the sea waves and the sharpness of a square - that symbolises a new canvas for Monique Amarante, this amazing all-around arquitect, to do her magic. The brand color palette is mainly black and white and a pinch of blue to make the link with the salty water.
