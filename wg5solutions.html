Client: WG5 Solutions - wg5solutions.com.br

Services: Visual Identity & Website Development

Year: 2019

WG5 Solutions is a Carioca company that brings solutions for the energy and infrastructure market. They needed a well structured visual identity that could show their know-how in this market niche plus a responsive and intuitive website to attrack new clients. The visual identity is a reference to boat canals together with the letter W from its name. The brand color palette is mainly shades of blue as request by the client and in allusion for the water.
