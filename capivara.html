Client: Capivara Bordados & Crafts

Services: Visual Identity

Year: 2018

Capivara Bordados & Crafts is the brand of Nana Behle that delights everybody with her handmade creations as ingenious as a capybara with a ball of thread! Nana always wanted a capybara wearing glasses logotype, so I made an illustration of a lovely and stylish one with red glasses holding a ball of thread. I kept the palette simple using black and red to give a strong touch to balance with the thin lines.
